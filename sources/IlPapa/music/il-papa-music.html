Music sources

The Il Papa manuscript has no music in it. So, I have made some up. I am not a musician, so I make no promises whatsoever about these. Please feel free to copy them, use them, change them, improve them, or whatever else you want. I would only ask that, if you improve upon them, please let me know what you've done.
