First, they take each other by the hand. And together they do the continenze and the reverenza. Then, thus holding hands, they continue by doing two scempi and 4 passetti. And when the four passetti have been done, the lady turns, and thus at the same time, they do the mezze riprese and the volta. The lady then gives her hand to him who is on her right hand, and he to her, and they do two scempi and 4 passetti rotating, and each of them returns to his place. And the lady does the same with him who is on her left hand. And thus, with each one returned to his place, they do the mezze riprese and the volta, and all this at the same time. Then the lady and the men go apart, each one going directly where they are facing, and they do two scempi and a doppio. And thus the men find themselves far from the lady. And with her shoulders she turns from them. Then all together, they do a passo falso, that is, a mezza ripresa under the left foot, and a passo on the right foot, which makes them turn their faces towards one another. Then all do the volta di lasso and the mezze riprese and the volta. Then they do opposite one another two scempi and a doppio, that is, three passi, and thus they find themselves again beside one another. And then they do a passo falso, in such a way that all of them come to turn the [illegible] And then all three of them another of the passi falsi, and they will turn to face with this. Then they do the mezze riprese and the volta. Then the lady gives her right hand to him who is on her right hand, and thus holding hands, they rotate [with] three trapassini and a passo, each one returning to his place. And when that is done, the lady gives her hand to the other man, and they do the same rotating with [?] trapassini and a passo. Then the do the mezze riprese and the volta. It continues with them doing a treccia of 8 trapassini, where as usual, the men change places, and the lady returns to her place. Then they do the mezze riprese, and the men do the volta, and in this same tempo, the lady enters their midst with 4 passetti. And it is... Finished.
