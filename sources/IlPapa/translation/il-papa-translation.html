Translation of the Il Papa Manuscript

Translation notes

There are a few notations in the text of the translation.

Line numbers are specified as triplets: A:B:C, where A specifies the leaf, B the page, and C the line within that page.

Also, where a new paragraph begins in the manuscript, we have bolded the first word of the new paragraph.
